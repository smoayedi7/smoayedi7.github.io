About Us

The Bodhi House is a place of collective creation and connection. We host various events, including art workshops, yoga sessions, film screenings, and gatherings of other sorts. The intention remains to be a space that fosters creativity and community. We also offer the space to our community members to host their own events and gatherings.

What Does Bodhi Mean?

Bodhi is a sanscrit word (Sanskrit: बोधि) that roughly translates to "awakening." The bodhi tree was the tree the Buddha spent hours meditating under. In Buddhism, the bodhi tree is a symbol of enlightenment or awakening. In our understanding, awakening is a processes of realizing all that is false, and connecting to the ever-present Truth.
